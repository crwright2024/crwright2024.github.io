Cordelia Wright

Wright is a Student at The Cleveland Institute of Art studing Industrial Design.

She has been making art in different mediums for the past few years. Her most common choice of design is charcoal and acrylic paint. Although most of her projects are drawings and or paintings, she enjoys to design elements of furniture as well. In the long run, Cordelia wants to pursue a career in the Industrial esign field.
